# Open-LMX Platform

Open-LMX Platform is the service layer built on top of the Open-LMX Foundation.

It operationalizes the open and interoperable education-to-employment model for learning, skills, assessment, certification, credentials, recruitment, hiring, growth, and career progression.

## Mission

Reduce friction between education and employment by giving learners, institutions, employers, recruiters, and industry bodies a trusted common operating model and interoperable platform.

## Problem

The education, employment, recruitment, and industry ecosystems lack a common language.

- Learners struggle to prove what they know and what they can do.
- Institutions teach curricula that may not map to real work.
- Employers repeatedly find graduates difficult to evaluate or not job ready.
- Recruiters lack trusted, portable, evidence-based skill signals.
- Industry capability needs are not consistently reflected in learning systems.

## Platform Purpose

Open-LMX Platform connects:

- Knowledge
- Learning
- Assessment

## Relationship to Foundation

The platform implements models and rules from:

```text
open-lmx/foundation
```

Foundation defines the common language.  
Platform operationalizes it as services, APIs, workflows, and user experiences.
